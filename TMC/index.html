Introduction

Explore the fundamental mechanistic principles of Ni-catalyzed activation of strong C-O bonds.

Nickel-catalyzed C-O bond activation represents a transformative approach in synthetic chemistry, enabling the conversion of abundant and renewable oxygenated feedstocks into valuable chemical products. This research area bridges fundamental organometallic chemistry with practical applications in sustainable synthesis.

Key Concepts

  • Oxidative addition of C-O bonds to nickel centers
  • Transmetalation and reductive elimination pathways
  • Ligand effects on reactivity and selectivity
  • Mechanistic studies using spectroscopic and computational methods

Learning Objectives

Understand the fundamental principles governing nickel-catalyzed reactions and develop intuition for predicting reaction outcomes based on substrate structure and reaction conditions.
