Introduction

Explore the fundamental mechanistic principles of Ni-catalyzed activation of strong C-O bonds.

Nickel-catalyzed C-O bond activation represents a transformative approach in synthetic chemistry, enabling the conversion of abundant and renewable oxygenated feedstocks into valuable chemical products. This research area bridges fundamental organometallic chemistry with practical applications in sustainable synthesis.
