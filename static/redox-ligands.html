Platform Overview

The Redox Active Ligands project is a comprehensive study into how nitrogen-based ligands control reactivity. This platform connects theoretical foundations with cutting-edge AI tools, allowing researchers to move from intuition-based design to data-driven discovery.

Research Pipeline

  • Overview — Dive into the electronic and steric tuning capabilities of Bipyridines, Phenanthrolines, and Oxazolines.
  • AI Optimization — Explore how we use Graph Neural Networks to predict ligand-metal binding energies and turnover frequencies.
  • Database — Search thousands of ligand candidates with pre-computed quantum descriptors like HOMO/LUMO energies.
  • Methodology — Review our DFT functional choices (B3LYP/def2-TZVP) and model validation benchmarks.
  • Publications — Access the primary literature detailing our "Super-Ligand" discoveries and benchmark results.
