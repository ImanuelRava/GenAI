Platform Overview

The NiCOBot project encompasses a suite of interconnected research modules designed to advance our understanding and application of nickel-catalyzed C-O bond activation. Each module serves a distinct purpose within the research workflow, from theoretical foundations through to experimental prediction and educational outreach.

Research Pipeline

  • Introduction — Learn the fundamental theory behind Ni-catalyzed C-O bond activation, including mechanistic principles and key literature
  • Data Extraction — Leverage NLP pipelines and PDF parsing tools to systematically extract reaction data from the scientific literature
  • Citation Network — Explore the interconnected web of publications through interactive citation graph analysis and visualization
  • NiCOBot Live — Access the AI-powered predictive model for real-time reaction outcome prediction and condition optimization
  • Chemistry Quiz — Reinforce your understanding of catalysis concepts and AI methodology through interactive quizzes
